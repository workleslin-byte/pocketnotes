Designed for people who think

Questions
that matter.

Asked enough times that they earned a place here. Each one a one-line answer — because the page is small for a reason.

Why paper?
Because attention is physical. Paper doesn't update mid-thought. Paper doesn't ask for your password.
Why small?
Constraint sharpens thought. A small page makes you choose. Brevity is the practice — not the limitation.
Why carry it?
Because ideas don't repeat themselves. The first thought is the rawest, and the rarest. The notebook earns its place in your bag the first time it catches one.
Why grid pages?
Grid lines hold structure loosely — for words, sketches, maps, and lists that turn into systems. Lined pages over-prescribe. Blank pages intimidate. Grid is the middle path.
What size are they?
A6 — 105 × 148 mm. Just bigger than your palm. Smaller than your phone. Built to fit a coat pocket, the back of a jeans pocket, or the gap in any bag.
How long does one notebook last?
Two months at one page a day. Two weeks at four. As long as the ideas keep coming. Most people finish their first one before they know it.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes. Built in India. Carried everywhere. Domestic ships within 48 hours; international takes 7–14 days depending on customs.
What's the return policy?
Try one. If it doesn't change how you think, send it back within 30 days. We'll refund the notebook — but not the writing.
Do you do gift wrapping?
Yes. A simple cream wrap with a single coral seal. No bows, no bows-on-bows, nothing that distracts from the object inside.
