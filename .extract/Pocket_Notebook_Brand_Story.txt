





Brand Story: For the Ones Who Catch the Spark

There’s a kind of person who thinks in sparks.
 Not always in full sentences.
 Not always in polished slides.
 Just… flashes.

A phrase.
 A diagram.
 A hook.
 A what-if.

And when that moment hits, they need somewhere for it to land.

We built these notebooks for them.

They’re not journals.
 Not daily planners.
 Not pastel affirmations wrapped in fake leather.
 They’re tools.

A6-sized, grid-lined, staple-bound, 52 pages short — because most good ideas don’t need 200.
 They fit your bag, your back pocket, your habit of pulling them out mid-conversation.

They’re made for people who think fast —
 Writers, founders, designers, illustrators, songwriters, product folks, daydreamers, night-scribblers.

And above all, for people who know one truth:
 Ideas don’t wait. Neither should you.

This is not stationery.
 This is your idea’s first address.
 The place where you park brilliance before the world is ready to see it.

Write like it matters.
 Scribble like no one’s watching.
 And carry a tool that respects the raw, unfinished parts of your thinking —
 Because that’s where the real magic starts.
