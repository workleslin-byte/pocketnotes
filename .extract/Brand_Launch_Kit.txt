


POCKET NOTES — FIGMA DESIGN BRIEF

Theme: A system for thinkers. A philosophy you can carry.
 Type: Philosophical school × Cult design × Interactive system
 Goal: Build a homepage that positions Pocket Notes as a discipline, not a product; as a method, not a brand.





🎨 DESIGN PRINCIPLES

Metaphor First — Every visual should feel like it stands in for a cognitive or philosophical idea.


Texture of Thought — Use matte gradients, subtle noise, neuro‑symbolic imagery (MRI scans, scribbles, paths, flow diagrams).


Spatial Design — Give every idea room to breathe; treat whitespace like silence in thought.


Aesthetic Tension — Combine structure (grids, system diagrams) with rawness (ink, chaos, fast scribbles).


Interaction as Initiation — Visitors must feel like they’re entering a school of thought, not browsing a store.
